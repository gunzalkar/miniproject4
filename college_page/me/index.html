About the University

MIT Art Design and Technology University (MIT ADT University) is a place where creativity and innovation coexist.

MIT ADT has been established under the flagship of the prestigious MIT Group of Institutions. It is a UGC recognized multidisciplinary University and has been bestowed with the ‘Best Campus Award’ by ASSOCHAM.

MIT ADT spans across a vast green stretch of 125 acres of land at Vishwaraj Baugh Loni, Kalbhor, Pune
